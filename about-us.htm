About Us

Located in the city of Ottawa, The Magic: the Gathering Store has been a staple of the community since 2010, offering the best vibes for any of your nerdy outlets like comics, action figures and card games.

We are an all inclusive hangout destination, and offer lots of great amenities and events such as our on-site cafe and our many Magic: the Gathering tournaments!
